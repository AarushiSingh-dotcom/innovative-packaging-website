ABOUT US

Innovative Packaging Industry is a manufacturer and supplier of high-quality packaging materials. We specialize in export-worthy corrugated boxes available in 3, 5, 7, and 9 ply variants.

We also provide:
- PP Sheet Boxes
- Partitions
- Poly Bags
- Angle Boards
- Stretch Wrap
- Brown Tape
- Sutli

The company established a plant for manufacturing corrugated boards and boxes to meet growing industry demands. Commercial production began in April 2017.
