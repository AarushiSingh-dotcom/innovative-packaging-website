About Us

INNOVAATIVE PACKAGING INDUSTRY

Exporters & Packaging Solutions Provider

INNOVAATIVE PACKAGING INDUSTRY is a dynamic and growing enterprise engaged in the manufacturing of packaging materials and the export of agro products, especially premium quality rice.

We combine strong industry knowledge with a commitment to quality to deliver reliable, hygienic, and cost-effective solutions.

Our Focus is to meet client expectations through consistency, timely delivery, and customized services.

  • To build long-term relationships with global clients.
  • To ensure customer satisfaction through reliability and consistency.
Our Solution

Hard Hardened Corrugated Board (HHCB)

HHCB is our flagship patented packaging material — a complete packaging solution that delivers the strength and durability industry demands, with the sustainability of paperboard. It has been recognized with 7 India Star Awards from the Indian Institute of Packaging (IIP), underlining its quality and innovation.

  • Patented material — Proprietary Hard Hardened Corrugated Board technology for superior strength and consistency.
  • Complete packaging solution — From design to production, we supply end-to-end packaging that protects your products and meets export and regulatory standards.
  • 7 India Star Awards (IIP) — Multiple national awards from the Indian Institute of Packaging for excellence in packaging design and performance.

Award-winning packaging across industries. Our solutions are trusted for safe delivery, seaworthy export packaging, and eco-friendly, biodegradable alternatives to wood. Backed by rigorous quality testing — including Bursting Strength, GSM, Moisture, and Caliper testing — we ensure every board meets the highest standards.
